Welcome to CrystalMaker Software Ltd, pioneers in interactive scientific software for research & teaching in chemistry, solid-state physics, materials science, mineralogy and crystallography. Use our products to build, display & manipulate all kinds of crystal or molecular structures - with photo-realistic 3D graphics. Animate structural behaviour, record movies and simulate diffraction properties for powders and single crystals.
