Career Information

Being a biologist can be a fulfilling and exciting profession provided your own personal goals, talents, and interests match the activities of biologists. The information on these pages provides general career information for a variety of interests and careers in biology. Explore this area and discover the diversity of biology, some tips about academic job interviews, and some useful links.

Frequently asked questions (FAQ) about careers and disciplinary career information was prepared by the SICB Educational Council and are found below. In addition, suggestions and information on job interviews and some useful links were prepared by the Student/Postdoctoral Affairs Committee.
