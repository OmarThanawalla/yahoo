Cavity QED Microlaser


Schematic of the microlaser. A: barium atomic beam (unexcited atoms shown in blue, excited atoms in red); P: pi-pulse excitation field; M1, M2: resonator mirrors; L: lens; D: photon counting detector; U: auxiliary output.
