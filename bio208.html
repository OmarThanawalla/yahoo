Transforming your data into information

We are experts in freshwater macroinvertebrate identification, having analyzed over 20,000 macroinvertebrate samples from streams, rivers, springs, wetlands, ponds and lakes throughout western North America.

Our technical services include field sampling, endangered invertebrate species surveys, and quantitative analysis of macroinvertebrate communities and fish diets.

We provide statistical data analysis in a variety of formats, interpretation of results to meet your needs, and prepared reports.
