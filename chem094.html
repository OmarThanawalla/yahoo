Natronx

NatronX understands the importance of air pollution control as well as the challenges utilities and industrial companies face with constantly changing environmental regulations. To meet these challenges NatronX provides innovative dry sorbent injection solutions and products that efficiently reduce air pollution (SO2, SO3, HCl, HF, Mercury) in coal-fired power plants, waste incinerators, industrial boilers and other industries, while reducing the significant capital investment required by environmental regulations.
