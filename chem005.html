What is ICIS?

ICIS is the world's largest petrochemical market information provider and has fast-growing energy and fertilizer divisions.

Our aim is to give companies a competitive advantage. We deliver trusted pricing data, high-value news, analysis and independent consulting, enabling our customers to make better-informed trading and planning decisions.

Our global team covers over 120 commodities across markets in Europe, Africa, the Middle East, Asia-Pacific and the Americas.
