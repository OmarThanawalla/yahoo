United Products & Instruments

United Products & Instruments was founded in the nineties and has grown steadily thanks to the uncompromising commitment to quality products & services and the support of our customers. Our technical and support staff are available to help you select the best products for your applications. United Products & Instruments carry a large inventory and service everything we sell in addition to offering repair and maintenance by factory technicians.
United Products & Instruments is open for business Monday – Friday, 8:30 am to 5:30 pm EST 52 weeks a year. We are closed for certain national holidays. On this website you will find a collection of products designed and developed for their quality, reliability, price and value.
United Products & Instruments products are backed by the industry’s best warrantees and are available from local and national distributors. Ask your supplier for United Products & Instruments brand products, or contact us for referral to a distributor.

New Product Development
United Products & Instruments has an aggressive new product development program consisting of digital microscopy systems, microscopes, centrifuges, lab equipment, mixers and more. Our new UNICOVUE digital microscopy systems include a selection of medium to high resolution cameras with state of the art image capture, editing and manipulation software. These systems are adaptable to existing microscopes (Monocular, Binocular and Trinocular); and our line will include integrated systems featuring microscopes with built-in cameras and software interfaces. You will come to appreciate the ease at which you can use these systems and the quality of imaging. Contact us for more information on our new digital systems.

Mission Statement
Our mission is to develop, manufacture and market through distributors; quality Laboratory products that any Laboratory Technician, Physician, Veterinarian, Teacher or Researcher would be proud to use in their lab.
Our customers will become sales advocates by telling their colleagues about the positive experiences and added value they received from using United Products & Instruments products.
We will focus on our customer’s applications and offer products targeted to their needs. United Products & Instruments products will be supported with easily accessible technical support and local manufacturer’s representatives ready and able to assist when needed.
