By being a member of CSMB (formerly CSBMCB), you will participate in building a strong community of scientists within Canada. This community plays an important role to foster interaction, communication and collaboration amongst discovery scientists, as well as build the next generation of researchers for academia, industry and the entrepreneurial enterprise.

Members Benefits Include:
  • Strong Collective Advocacy Voice
  • Special Reduced Conference Rates
  • Access to Travel Awards for Trainees
  • CSMB Publications
  • Eligibility for Awards and Prizes
  • Networking within the CSMB Scientific Community

For more information, or to discuss potential topics for upcoming conferences, or to raise issues pertinent to our community, contact a CSMB Regional Representative, or a member of the Board of Directors.
