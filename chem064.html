Welcome to Harvard University’s Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology (CCB). We are proud to continue our department’s rich tradition of research and scholarship in the molecular sciences. Our research activities in CCB span the core disciplines of organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry and the interdisciplinary frontiers of the life and physical sciences.

Park Group Collaboration Creates Nanothermometer

August 23, 2013

Working with colleagues in the Physics Department, the Park group has developed a new tool for thermal sensing in living cells by exploiting the ground-state energy gap in the nitrogen vacancy centers found in some diamonds, as reported in Nature News.

Bioinspired Coating from Aizenberg Group

August 19, 2013

The Aizenberg group reports a new transparent coating inspired by the properties of the ultra slippery surface of the leaves of the carnivorous pitcher plant.
