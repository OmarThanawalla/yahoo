General information

University of Helsinki has concentrated its basic and applied biological research and teaching to the Viikki Campus located some 10 kilometres north east from the city center. To foster research in molecular biosciences between research groups in different organizations, a research group organization was established in 1996. It is organized to research programs with their own seminars and other activities.

Dissertations carried out in participating groups are published in Dissertationes Biocentri Viikki Universitatis Helsingiensis.
