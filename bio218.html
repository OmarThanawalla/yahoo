Welcome to the companion Web site to the NOVA program "Night Creatures of the Kalahari," originally broadcast on January 6, 1998. This program displays the hidden world of rare and exotic animals beneath the grassland plains of the Kalahari. Here's what you'll find online:
  • Night Vision
    Where we humans see only darkness, some animals can see their next meal. How do they do it?

  • Zoology After Dark
    It can be difficult enough to study wild animals that are active in the day. How are zoologists able to study nocturnal creatures?
