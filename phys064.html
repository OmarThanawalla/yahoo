  • Hypres, Inc. manufactures superconducting microelectronics including superconducting Integrated Circuits (ICs). Its products include voltage standard circuits and systems, wide bandwidth semiconductor-based amplifiers, and superconducting circuit foundry service. The company was founded in 1983 and is based in Elmsford, New York.
