Welcome to the ACB Web Site


The Association for Clinical Biochemistry and Laboratory Medicine (ACB) was founded in 1953 and is celebrating its Diamond Jubilee this year. The ACB is one of the oldest such Associations in the world. Based in the United Kingdom, it is a professional body dedicated to the practice and promotion of clinical science. The Association has medical and non-medical members in all major UK healthcare laboratories, in many university departments and in several commercial companies. The links with its Corporate Members leads to a fruitful relationship with the clinical diagnostics industry. The Association liaises with and is consulted by many national and international organisations on issues relating to Laboratory Medicine.
