Folia Parasitologica is a quarterly published international journal that covers all branches of animal parasitology, including morphology, taxonomy, biology, biochemistry, physiology, immunology and molecular biology of parasites, host-parasite relationships and parasite evolution. The language of publication is English. Suitable manuscripts are reviewed by at least two independent referees before acceptance.
The current ISI Impact Factor: IP = 2.515.

Last published issue: 60(3) July 2013
Advanced on-line published: ahead-of-print

Offered to the authors:
  • prompt evaluation of manuscripts and advanced on-line publication of papers ahead of print
  • publication free-of-charge
  • excellent reproduction of illustrations
  • wide visibility of published papers by open access
  • free-of-charge permission to reproduce illustrations in subsequent publications
