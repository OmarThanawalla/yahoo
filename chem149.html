A Three-Pronged Template Approach for Rapid HPLC Method Development
HPLC method development is an arduous process requiring considerable experience and scientific judgement. In this article, a roadmap for rapid HPLC method development using a three-pronged approach is presented.
Screening by LC–MS-MS and Fast Chromatography: An Alternative Approach to Traditional Immunoassays?
A new technique was developed based on liquid chromatography coupled with tandem mass spectrometry to analyze drugs that are not part of current immunoassay screening, specifically zolpidem, zopiclone, and zaleplone, or "z-drugs." This method allows for the simultaneous quantification of the z-drugs and all major benzodiazepines in human urine samples.
