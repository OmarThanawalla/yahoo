STABLE ISOTOPE ANALYSIS OF 2H, 13C, 15N, 18O & 34S

Iso-Analytical is a modern laboratory specializing in Stable Isotope Ratio Analysis (SIRA). We provide a high quality analytical service for the light stable isotopes: hydrogen-2, carbon-13, nitrogen-15, oxygen-18 and sulfur-34. Established since 2000, we have analysed in excess of 200,000 samples from over 3,500 individual studies in agriculture, archaeology, ecology, environmental science, food authenticity, forensic chemistry, geochemistry and medical research. We pride ourselves in providing a quality assured service, in good time and at a competitive price.

Our range of stable isotope analytical services may be viewed by isotope or application. To obtain a price for isotopic analysis of your samples simply complete our quote form and we will get back to you with an all inclusive price.

If you operate an isotope ratio mass spectrometer for hydrogen, carbon, nitrogen, oxygen or sulfur analysis, you may be interested in our range of laboratory standards or standard calibration service.
