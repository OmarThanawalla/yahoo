RECOMMENDED RESOURCE:
Organic Chemistry I for Dummies is an easy-to-understand, fun introduction to organic chemistry. While material coverage is not as in-depth as a textbook, this book explains the principles of organic chemistry in simple terms and includes worked-out problems to help readers get up to speed on the basics. Highlights include how to decipher "organic-speak", how to master chemical reactions, and how to develop strategies for solving complex problems.
