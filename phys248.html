Photos of the Month

Buy this photo


Over 400 years ago in August of 1609, Galileo demonstrated his first telescope to the Venetian Senate. Galileo based his version of the optical instrument on spyglasses that had been developed the previous year by Dutch spectacle makers. Since then, telescopes have been offering us a glimpse into the unknown. Optical, radio, and solar telescopes are just some of the innovations developed since Galileo's time! For the month of August we've created a spectacle of telescopes from our historical photos collection. To see more images in our collection like the ones we've selected, type the keyword “telescopes” into our Quick Search, or click here.
