GHS Is Coming

In March 2012, OSHA issued the first major change to their Hazard Communication Standard since 1985 to include provisions of the Globally Harmonized System (GHS) for the Classification and Labeling of Chemicals. This will change everything - all MSDS (now SDS), employee training, written compliance programs and more. Be prepared! Contact EHSI for assistance.
