Research Journal of Chemistry and Environment

An International Research Journal of Chemical and Environmental Sciences.


This is a Magazine that covers Natural and Physical Sciences, Environmental Sciences, Earth Sciences and Atmospheric Sciences  Chemistry, Bio-chemistry, Physics, Meteorology, Geography, Geology, Botany, Zoology, Chemical Engineering, Civil and Environmental Engineering together

 

A Journal which has made the unique effort to bring together under one cover, Researchers in Chemistry and Environment and their deeply significant inter-relationship. The journal emphasizes the interdependence and inter-relationship between chemical science and environmental sciences.
