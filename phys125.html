Overview

The Chaos Research Group studies deterministic chaos and nonlinear dynamics in engineering systems and is located within the College of Engineering at the University of Tennessee. This group closely collaborates with the researchers at the Oak Ridge National Laboratory.

The Chaos Research Group is an interdisciplinary team of faculty, students and engineering staff which studies the nature of complex, nonlinear engineering systems to achieve a better understanding of the underlying physical processes, to develop better diagnostics, or to control these systems in situations in which conventional control might be infeasible or too costly.
