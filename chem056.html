Science Center


The Chlorine Science Center strives to provide teachers, students and home-schoolers with resources to help improve the way scientific and environmental issues are discussed in the classroom. The Chlorine Science Center features great lesson plans, classroom activities, articles and downloadable coloring books.

The Chlorine Science Center is sponsored by the Chlorine Chemistry Division of the American Chemistry Council (ACC) and represents an effort to help bring objective and balanced discussion of science and risk issues to classrooms around the country. Working with premier education organizations like the Science Education for Public Understanding Program (SEPUP) and Project Learning Tree (PLT), ACC's Chlorine Chemistry Division helps equip educators with the knowledge and tools needed to educate students.
