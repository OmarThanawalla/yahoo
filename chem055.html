Scientific Notation

It is sometimes convenient to express large or small numbers in scientific notation.
For Example: 5,000 = 5 x 103 and .0004 = 4 x 10- 4.

Commonly used prefixes for powers of 10 used with metric units are given below in Table 1.1.


 
Table 2.1: Prefixes used with metric units.
Power Prefix Abbreviation
10- 9 nano n
10- 6 micro
10- 3 milli m
10- 2 centi c
10- 1 deci d
103 kilo k
106 mega M

For Example:
a) 60,000 m = 6 x 104 m = 60 km
b) 0.003 s = 3 x 10- 3 s = 3 ms
