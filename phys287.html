Since 1987, Photonics, Inc. has been working extensively in the areas of photonics, semiconductors and fiber optic devices and sensors.

Photonics, Inc. has two affiliates, Photonics Laboratories, Inc. and Photonics GTT (Global Technology Transfer), Inc. Photonics Laboratories, Inc. is a Research & Development company challenged with developing cutting-edge technology in photonics, semiconductors and fiber optics. Photonics GTT, Inc. specializes in international technology transfer.

The photonics and fiber optics technologies have expanded rapidly in the past several years for a broad spectrum of applications, from communication systems, optical imaging and networking, to biomedical, industrial and military devices and sensors.
